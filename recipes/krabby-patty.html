Krabby Patty

Description:

The Krabby Patty is the Krusty Krab's signature and most popular food item. It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty; it can also be ordered with cheese and or, pickles. Created by Mr. Krabs and Plankton.

Ingredients:

  • Krabby Patty
  • Sea Sesame seeds
  • Sea Bottom bun
  • Sea Ketchup
  • Sea Mustard
  • Sea Mayonnaise
  • Sea Relish
  • Sea cheese
  • Sea pickles
  • Sea lettuce
  • Sea tomatoes
  • Sea onions
  • Sea Tartar sauce
  • Sea Secret ingredient
  • Sea "Chopped Love"
  • Sea Top bun

Steps:

  1. Start with the Sesame seed bun
  2. Then, place your Patty on bottom bun.
  3. Next, place your cheese, lettuce, pickles, tomato and any condiments you like, dont forget the secret ingredient of course.
  4. Lastly, complete the Krabby Patty by placing the top bun
